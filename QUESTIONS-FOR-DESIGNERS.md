# Questions for the Designers

Hey! We're getting ready to build Chess Gold. A few questions about your game modes before we start on the later features.

---

## Two Kings (Loot Boxes mode)

If you get a king from a loot box, you now have 2 kings. We're thinking it works like this:

- Your opponent has to **capture both kings** to win (like having 2 lives)
- You don't HAVE to protect both — if one gets taken, you're back to normal
- When you're down to your last king, normal chess rules kick in again

**Does that sound right, or did you have something different in mind?**

---

## Siege

You said pieces on center squares get upgraded "for their next move" when the pulse happens.

**Does the upgrade last for just 1 move, or until the next pulse?**

---

## Flashlight

1. **What can you see?** We're thinking: you can see your own pieces + every square they could move to + whatever piece is blocking their path. Everything else is dark. **Sound right?**

2. **Flashlight Gold combo:** When you're placing a new piece from the shop, can you place it on a square you can't see? Or only on squares you can see?

---

## Gold Mine

You said "you can do ANYTHING" — just want to make sure we get it right:

1. Do you start with gold, or is everything free?
2. Can you place pieces anywhere on the board (not just the first 3 rows)?
3. Is there gold income per turn?
4. When you said "take and move 1 piece every turn" — does "take" mean capture an enemy piece? Or place a new piece?
